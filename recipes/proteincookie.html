Protein Cookie

Description

These protein cookies are packed in with 25 grams of protein! Perfect for any health conscious person!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp protein powder (16g)
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter (30g)
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sweetener of choice
  • 1 tbsp water
  • 1/4 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/16 tsp each: baking soda and salt
  • optional chocolate chips, chopped peanuts, etc.

Steps

  1. Preheat an oven or toaster oven to 350 F.
  2. If not already soft, gently warm peanut butter until easily stir-able. Mix dry ingredients in a small bowl, then add wet to form a cookie dough.
  3. If using a granulated sweetener, add an additional tablespoon of water.
  4. Press into a cookie shape.
  5. Bake 7 minutes, then let cool completely before handling, because the cookie firms up as it cools.
